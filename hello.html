Helen Kerr was diagnosed with schizophrenia in 2004 and hopes to inspire others with similar diagnoses that they too can achieve their dreams.

About the book

It's a journey with true diary entries from different viewpoints in this life experience, through the true eyes of everyone involved

Creation of the book

I started writing this book as I had some free time, and it was a way of sorting through my stress and gave me a purpose. 
Soon after starting with my diary entries, I decided it was my goal to put it between two covers, as it's for anyone who wants to write a book or achieve their dreams in any way. 

Who would benefit from this book?

A person with mental disabilities will be able to find comfort by resonating with the experiences in this book.
People living with, or have relationships with other mentally disabled or schizophrenic people will be able to understand them better and the way they think and perceive things
